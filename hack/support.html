Frequently Asked Questions

How do I raise a public issue? ▼
Go to Raise Request on your dashboard, select the issue category, fill in the details with location, and submit.
How can I track my complaint? ▼
Go to the "Track Complaints" section. You will see all your submitted issues and their current live status.
Who can see my complaint? ▼
Your complaint is first verified by a Moderator and then assigned to the relevant Public Representative for your district.
How long does resolution take? ▼
Resolution time depends on the priority and nature of the issue, typically ranging from 3 to 15 working days.
